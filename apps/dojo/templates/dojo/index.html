Welcome to the Django ORM Dojo!

This app is designed to help you practice database queries using Django's ORM syntax. The database consists of three models, League, Team and Player. At the top of each page will be three tables generated from all the records in each model which you can use for reference. When you submit a query, the app will show you the results of your query and whether or not it retrieved the expected records from the database. If the query generated an error, it will show the error. The yellow belt exam starts off with simple "find" queries. The red belt exam deals with ForeignKey relationships. Finally, the black belt exam tests your knowledge of ManyToMany relationships. Have fun!
